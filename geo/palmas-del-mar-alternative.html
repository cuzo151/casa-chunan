A Quieter Alternative to Palmas del Mar Hotels and Resorts

Casa Chunan gives travelers a private southeast Puerto Rico stay in Maunabo, close enough for regional day trips but calmer than a resort-style base.

Palmas del Mar works for travelers who want a planned resort community. Casa Chunan works for travelers who want a private home, a quieter town, and more local-feeling access to Puerto Rico’s southeast coast.

LocationMaunabo, southeast Puerto Rico near Punta Tuna Lighthouse and Playa Los Bohíos.

When Casa Chunan is the better fit

Choose Casa Chunan if your group values privacy, a private pool, a kitchen, multiple bedrooms, local drives, and a less-commercial travel pace.

When Palmas may be the better fit

Choose Palmas del Mar if your priority is a resort community with centralized amenities, golf, marina activity, and a more self-contained development.

The southeast coast advantage

From Maunabo, guests can explore Punta Tuna Lighthouse, Playa Los Bohíos, Playa Larga, Patillas, Yabucoa, Humacao, and other southeast-coast stops while returning to a private home at night.

Quick Answers for Travelers

Is Maunabo near Palmas del Mar?

Maunabo and Palmas del Mar are both on Puerto Rico’s eastern/southeastern side. Casa Chunan is a Maunabo option for travelers who prefer a quieter private rental over a resort-community stay.

Is Casa Chunan a resort?

No. Casa Chunan is a private vacation rental, not a resort. That means more privacy and home-style space, but guests should not expect resort-front desk services.

Why choose Maunabo instead of a resort area?

Maunabo offers a quieter local pace, scenic coastline, lighthouse access, and a strong sense of place for travelers who want a less crowded Puerto Rico trip.
