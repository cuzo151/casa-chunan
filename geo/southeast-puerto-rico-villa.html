Southeast Puerto Rico is the island's best-kept secret. Away from the heavy resort traffic of San Juan and the crowded beaches of the west coast, the southeast offers a more authentic, peaceful Puerto Rican experience. Casa Chunan is a 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom private villa located in Maunabo, perfectly positioned where the mountains meet the Caribbean Sea.

Whether you are planning a family vacation, a remote work retreat, or a quiet getaway, this southeast Puerto Rico villa provides the space, privacy, and local access you need. You're minutes from Playa Los Bohíos, the historic Punta Tuna Lighthouse, and some of the best local seafood on the island.
