The Punta Tuna Lighthouse (Faro de Punta Tuna) is one of Puerto Rico's most striking historic landmarks, built in 1892 and still guiding ships today. When you stay at Casa Chunan, you are booking a private villa just minutes from this iconic site.

Our 3-bedroom vacation rental offers a quiet, residential base for exploring the lighthouse, the adjacent nature reserve, and the pristine, uncrowded sands of Playa Punta Tuna. Enjoy morning coffee with mountain views, spend the afternoon exploring the lighthouse grounds, and return to your private pool for the evening.
