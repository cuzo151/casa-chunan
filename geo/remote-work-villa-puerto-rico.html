Why Maunabo works for workations

Maunabo is quieter than resort and city hubs, which helps travelers keep a routine. Mornings can be work-focused; late afternoons can be beach, lighthouse, pool, or local dinner time.

Space beats a hotel room

A 3-bedroom home lets one guest take calls while another cooks, rests, or uses a separate room. For couples or small teams, the layout is more practical than one hotel room.

Is Puerto Rico good for remote work?

Puerto Rico is a practical Caribbean workation choice for U.S. travelers because it uses U.S. currency, U.S. mobile carriers often work well, and no passport is needed for U.S. citizens.
