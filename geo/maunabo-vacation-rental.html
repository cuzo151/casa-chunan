Why stay in Maunabo?

Maunabo sits on Puerto Rico’s southeast coast, away from the densest resort corridors. Travelers come here for Punta Tuna Lighthouse, Playa Los Bohíos / Playa Negra, Playa Larga, the Punta Tuna wetlands, seafood kiosks, and the relaxed pace of a small coastal town.

Why Casa Chunan works as your base

The home gives guests 3 bedrooms, a private pool, indoor-outdoor gathering space, air conditioning, parking, and an easy drive to beaches and day trips. For families or small groups, it creates a more flexible stay than splitting into hotel rooms.

Who should book it?

Casa Chunan is best for families, couples who want privacy, friends traveling together, remote workers, and visitors who want a quieter alternative to San Juan, Rincón, or resort-only Puerto Rico itineraries.
