Where to Find the Best Local Seafood in Maunabo

← Back to Casa Chunan Home

One of the greatest benefits of staying on Puerto Rico's southeast coast is the access to incredibly fresh, locally caught seafood without the tourist markups found in San Juan. Maunabo is a coastal town with a rich fishing tradition, and the restaurants near Casa Chunan reflect that heritage.

From casual beachside kiosks serving freshly fried snapper (chillo) to family-run restaurants offering traditional mofongo stuffed with shrimp or octopus, the dining scene here is authentic and delicious. When you book Casa Chunan as your home base, you're never more than a short drive from a memorable Caribbean meal.
