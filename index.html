Discover Maunabo

What's Nearby

Puerto Rico's secret southeast coast — real, raw, and breathtaking.

5 min away

Playa Los Bohíos

Calm turquoise waters, white sand, almost no crowds. A hidden gem locals still call their own.

10 min away

Punta Tuna Lighthouse

A 19th-century lighthouse perched on dramatic rocky cliffs. Sunsets here are otherworldly.

1 hr away

El Yunque Rainforest

The only tropical rainforest in the US National Forest system — waterfalls, mist, and pure magic.
