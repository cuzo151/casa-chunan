Our Story

A New York dream,
planted in Puerto Rico

After 30 years in New York's tech industry, Kimlee left it all behind — trading office towers for palm trees, bumper-to-bumper traffic for coquí frogs, and grey skies for golden Caribbean sunsets.

The result is Casa Chunan: a hand-crafted sanctuary in Maunabo, where the lush mountains of Puerto Rico roll right down to the Caribbean sea. Every detail has been curated with love.

Discover Maunabo

Hidden between mountains
& the Caribbean Sea

Puerto Rico's secret southeast coast — real, raw, and breathtaking.

5 min away

Playa Los Bohíos

Calm turquoise waters, white sand, almost no crowds. A hidden gem locals still call their own.

10 min away

Punta Tuna Lighthouse

A 19th-century lighthouse perched on dramatic rocky cliffs. Sunsets here are otherworldly.

1 hr away

El Yunque Rainforest

The only tropical rainforest in the US National Forest system — waterfalls, mist, and pure magic.
