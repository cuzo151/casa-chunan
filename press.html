About the Property

Casa Chunan is a 3-bedroom luxury vacation rental on the southeast coast of Puerto Rico, in the small coastal town of Maunabo. The property includes a private pool, ocean and mountain views, and is a 5-minute drive from Playa Los Bohíos and a 10-minute drive from the 19th-century Punta Tuna Lighthouse.

The villa is set in a quieter corner of Puerto Rico that most visitors never see — the rain-shadow side of the Cordillera Central, where the mountains breathe down into the Caribbean. It is a 90-minute drive from San Juan International Airport (SJU) and a world away in pace.

About the Host

Kimlee Gely was born and raised in Puerto Rico and currently lives in Maunabo. She is a registered Airbnb Superhost, rated 5.0 by every guest who has reviewed the property. She personally writes the travel guides on the site, hosts every guest, and answers most inquiries within an hour.

Long (300 words)

Casa Chunan is a luxury 3-bedroom Airbnb in Maunabo, Puerto Rico — the corner of the island that the cruise ships and the resort circuit have not yet found. Hosted by Kimlee Gely, an Airbnb Superhost rated 5.0 by every guest, the villa offers a private pool, ocean and mountain views, an open-air terraza, a fully equipped kitchen, three private bedrooms, and quiet that does not exist on the north coast.

Maunabo sits on the southeast coast, in the rain shadow of the Cordillera Central. The local beach, Playa Los Bohíos, is a 5-minute drive. The historic Punta Tuna Lighthouse — built 1893, still operating — is 10 minutes away, with a coastal walking path and a sea-turtle nesting beach below. El Yunque, Puerto Rico's only tropical rainforest, is 45 minutes north. Old San Juan is 90 minutes northwest.

The villa sleeps up to six and starts at $172/night. Direct flights from NYC (JFK/LGA/EWR), Newark, Philadelphia, Boston, Chicago, and other US East Coast hubs reach San Juan in about 3.5 hours. No passport is needed for US travelers. US dollar, US cell service, US healthcare standards.
