Inside Casa Chunan: Where Modern Design Meets Tropical Living

Multiple bedrooms designed as private sanctuaries. A fully equipped designer kitchen flowing into outdoor living space. A private pool surrounded by lush landscaping — yours alone, no crowds, no reserved chairs. High-speed WiFi for remote workers. Coastal views from multiple points in the home.

Why Puerto Rico Is the Smartest Luxury Destination

No passport required for US citizens. US currency, US cell plans, 2-4 hour flights from most major cities. 500+ years of layered history, extraordinary food culture, and the best winter weather in the Caribbean — warm days, low humidity, minimal rain from December through April.

What to Do When You Are Not Poolside

  • Beaches: Flamenco Beach in Culebra, hidden surf breaks, calm snorkeling bays
  • Food: Roadside lechon stands to upscale San Juan restaurants, mofongo, coffee haciendas
  • Culture: Old San Juan cobblestone streets, El Morro fortress, Santurce street art, live salsa
  • Nature: El Yunque rainforest, bioluminescent bay kayak tours, waterfalls

Who Casa Chunan Is Perfect For

  • Couples: Private pool, outdoor dining, quiet mornings with coastal views
  • Families: Multiple bedrooms, kitchen freedom, pool entertainment
  • Groups: Milestone birthdays, bachelorettes, reunions — space to gather
  • Digital nomads: High-speed WiFi, workspaces, Puerto Rico tax incentives (Act 60)
  • Hotel escapees: A real home, not a room — your fridge, your playlist, your schedule

Planning Tips

  • Book high season (Dec-Apr) months in advance

  • Rent a car for coastal drives and mountain exploration
  • Pack light, warm weather only, sunscreen mandatory
